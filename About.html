The Wonders of Honey

A natural sweetener made by bees!

What is Honey?

Honey is a sweet, golden liquid produced by bees from the nectar of flowers. It has been used for thousands of years as a food and a medicine.

Benefits of Honey

  • Natural sweetener
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Helps with wound healing
  • Soothes sore throats

Types of Honey

Honey comes in many varieties, depending on the flowers the bees collect nectar from. Common types include:

  • Clover Honey
  • Manuka Honey
  • Acacia Honey
  • Wildflower Honey
